FIRST Scholarship FAQ

Q: How do I apply to U of M's FIRST scholarship?
A: When you apply to the University of Michigan College of Engineering Ann Arbor, you are automatically considered if you mention FIRST in your application.

Q: What is the deadline for application?
A: Since the application is the same as your application to the University, the deadline is the same as that due date.

Q: Who does this scholarship apply to?
A: This scholarship applies to anyone who has participated in a FIRST Robotics program and is attending University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. Preference is given to in-state, need-based applicants.

If your question is not answered here, please send an email to famnm@umich.edu.
