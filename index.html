What is FIRST®?

FIRST® (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a non-profit organization intended to get K-12 children involved in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Founded in 1989, it now reaches over 400,000 kids worldwide. Learn More >
