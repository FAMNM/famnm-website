What is FAMNA?

FAMNA is a University of Australia student organization devoted to organizing student and departmental efforts to support the FIRST® program locally and throughout the country of Australia. FAMNA offers college students the opportunity to volunteer in the various FIRST programs and events, provides funding for teams, and acts as a liaison between teams and various university departments.
