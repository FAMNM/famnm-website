FAMNM FIRST® Tech Challenge Kickoff

Watch the kickoff broadcast, tour U-M robotics labs, inspect the POWER PLAY game field in person, and more on Saturday, September 10th, 2022 at the University of Michigan's North Campus.

Register Team Register Participant

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is the middle/high school level in the FIRST progression of programs. In 2017, FAMNM launched its first FTC Kickoff event, where we invited local teams to view the reveal of the new year's challenge in early September. The event came complete with FRC Demos, inspiring speeches, and a chance to see an official FTC game field!
