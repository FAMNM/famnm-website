Robot in 3 Days Team

FAMNM established their Robot in 3 Days project team in the 2018-2019 school year. Starting the day of FRC Kickoff, this team of students spends 36 hours to construct a robot that can compete in the new FRC game! The team then competed at the FIRST® Alumni Collegiate Competition, winning the competition and becoming the FIRST EVER STATE CHAMPS!
