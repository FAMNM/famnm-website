FAMNM Robot Quick Build

The Robot Quick Build (RQB) is an event held in early January, usually the day after our FRC Kickoff event. Teams work with FAMNM members to jumpstart their FRC season by getting a working drivetrain on the first day of build season! FAMNM offers assistance in mechanical, electrical, programming, and game strategy. Check out some highlights from this year's RQB below!
