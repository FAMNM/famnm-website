2019 FAMNM Robotics Day

About FAMNM Robotics Day

The FIRST Alumni and Mentors Network at Michigan (FAMNM) will host a Robotics Day on the North Campus of the University of Michigan on November 2, 2019. We want to create a fun and educational experience for the local FRC community. This is a day to learn about robotics and have fun with other teams in the area!

In the morning we will have workshops and presentations for students and mentors to attend. Topics will range from technical to interpersonal skills. Then in the afternoon, we will host a variety of special activities that explore the University and challenge students!
