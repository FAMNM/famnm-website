FAMNM Robotics Forums

The FAMNM Robotics Forums is an annual event hosted on the U of M Campus for teams to come and build their FIRST® Robotics knowledge. The FAMNM Forums is an annual event hosted on the U of M campus for teams to come and build their FIRST Robotics knowledge. The event features seminars on a wide range of topics, including mechanical design, strategy, team organization, volunteering, FIRST® alumni opportunities, and more! FAMNM is always looking for feedback from teams on what seminars they’d like to see!

2017 FAMNM Robotics Forums

FAMNM is excited to host our second annual FAMNM Forums event! We're currently in the planning stages, and will be using all of the great feedback we've received to make this event bigger and better. Check back closer to the Fall for more information!
