2018 FAMNM Forums

FAMNM is excited to be hosting our third annual FAMNM Forums event! The event will not only be hosted on the University of Michigan's North Campus but also livestreamed on our official Twitch account (see below).

For those who will be attending in person, you may park in the Stamps lot and check in at the west entrance to the DOW building. Presentations will be in DOW 1013, with refreshments served in DOW 1010 during the noon lunch break.

About FAMNM Forums

The FAMNM Robotics Forums is an annual event hosted on the U of M Campus for teams to come and build their FIRST® Robotics knowledge. The event features seminars on a wide range of topics, including mechanical design, strategy, team organization, volunteering, FIRST® alumni opportunities, and more! FAMNM is always looking for feedback from teams on what seminars they’d like to see!
