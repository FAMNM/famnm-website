FAMNM FIRST® Tech Challenge Kickoff

The FIRST® Tech Challenge (FTC) season begins each year in early September with a worldwide kickoff. At this time, the year’s challenge is revealed and teams begin their design and build season. Kickoffs provide an opportunity for teams to work with each other, brainstorm ideas, learn valuable skills through workshops, and interact with a full FTC game field.
