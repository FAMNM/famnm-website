Frequently Asked Questions

What gear ratio did you use on the arm?
We had a 100:1 gearbox driving a chain-and-sprocket mechanism with a ratio of roughly 3:1, so overall the ratio was 300:1.
What size gas strut did you use to counterbalance the arm?
We used a 30-pound gas strut.
