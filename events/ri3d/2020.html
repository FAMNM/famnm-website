Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my question posted here?
Reach out to us on Chief Delphi or at famnm.ri3d@umich.edu
Will you be releasing full robot CAD?
No, we aim to inspire teams and not for our design to be copied. There's much that can be improved upon in our design. If CAD for a specific component would be useful in your design process, reach out to us on Chief Delphi or at famnm.ri3d@umich.edu
Where can I find more Michigan Ri3D?
We have all sorts of content from the 2020 Ri3D season on our YouTube and Twitch accounts.
