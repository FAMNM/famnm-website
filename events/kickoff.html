FAMNM FIRST® Robotics Competition Kickoff

FAMNM held a kickoff event in person for FRC's 2023 season game, CHARGED UP, on Saturday, January 7th, 2023 at the University of Michigan's North Campus. The kickoff featured an engineering student organization fair, an info session about the University of Michigan's new Undergraduate Robotics program, guest speakers, and team breakout rooms, with more than 40 teams in attendance. Good luck, teams!
