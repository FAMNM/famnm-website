2017 FIRST® Robotics Kickoff

University of Michigan - Ann Arbor

Saturday, January 7, 2017

The FIRST Alumni and Mentors Network at Michigan (FAMNM) is excited to host our second annual Kickoff event! Based on feedback from our last Kickoff, we are hosting sessions ranging from seminars to quick builds depending on participant interest. After registering for the event, be sure to check your email for surveys about potential team development opportunities.

Our first event of the school year is the first annual FAMNM Robotics Forums. On October 15th, teams and individuals looking to build and share their FIRST® experience will meet on University of Michigan’s North Campus. More information can be found here.

Additionally, we are hosting a Quick Build event after the 2017 Kickoff. More details to come, but keep an eye out for an interest list that will be posted on this page.

Can’t wait for Kickoff? Check out the official FIRST® teaser!
