Welcome to FAMNM!
/'fam/
noun
1. A student organization at the University of Michigan designed to get students on campus involved in FIRST®, as well as support local FIRST® teams and students in the Ann Arbor area.

Are you a FIRST® team looking for resources or mentors? You've come to the right place! FAMNM is here to help you by connecting you to U of M students, machinery, and opportunities. As our member base is primarily comprised of FIRST® alumni, we do our best to provide the next generation of FIRST® students the best experience possible and grow our extensive FIRST® FAMNMily!

Any team that would like to obtain support and resources from FAMNM must become affiliated with FAMNM by working with one of our Coordinators to review and customize a contract. This will outline the expectations and responsibilities of both parties so as to ensure that FAMNM is best meeting the team’s needs and structure.

To facilitate your communication with our Coordinators, please fill out this Survey​ to let us know what type of support you are looking for.

FRC

For our FRC teams, we can help provide water jet access here at U of M, and mentor support throughout the build and competition season.

Interested? Email our head FRC Coordinator Alycia Gerber at famnm.FRC­-coord@umich.edu​ to get connected.

FTC

Currently, we are working on building our team relations within this division of FIRST, and do not have any resources available for support. However, as our organization grows we plan to expand our outreach to FTC teams as well.

Interested in getting involved? Email our Head Coordinator Spencer Marshall at famnm.head­-coord@umich.edu​ to get connected.

FLL

With FLL, we are working on building team relationships, and are currently only providing mentor support for teams in our area.

Interested? Email our FLL Coordinator Angelo Vozza at ​famnm.FLL-­coord@umich.edu to get connected.


For any other questions or inquiries please contact our Head Coordinator Spencer Marshall at ​famnm.head-­coord@umich.edu​ or our President Alex Guiamet at famnm.pres@umich.edu​.
