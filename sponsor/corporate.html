Supporting FAMNM as a Corporation

Are you a {{ FIRST }} supporter looking to connect to {{ FIRST }} Alumni on campus? Often, {{ FIRST }} supporters invest a lot into the program but have a hard time finding and recruiting {{ FIRST }} alumni. Let FAMNM connect you to our network of {{ FIRST }} alumni at the University of Michigan. Contact our Director of Corporate Outreach at famnm.corporate@umich.edu​.

Getting Involved as a Professional

Are you interested in mentoring a team or volunteering at an event? Contact our coordinators at the appropriate address: famnm.frc@umich.edu​ for high school robotics, famnm.ftc@umich.edu​ for middle school robotics, famnm.fll@umich.edu​ for elementary robotics, and famnm.volunteer@umich.edu​ to volunteer with us at any level.
