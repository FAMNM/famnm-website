Supporting FAMNM as a Corporation

Are you a FIRST® supporter looking to connect to FIRST Alumni on campus? Often, FIRST® supporters invest a lot into the program but have a hard time finding and recruiting FIRST® alumni. Let FAMNM connect you to our network of FIRST® alumni at the University of Michigan. Contact our Vice President of Corporate Outreach at famnm.co-vp@umich.edu​.

Getting Involved as a Professional

Are you interested in mentoring a team or volunteering at an event? Contact our Head Coordinator at famnm.head-coord@umich.edu​.
